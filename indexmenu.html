Cook It Just Like Moms

1. Chips n Grilled Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Irish potatoes
  • Chicken breasts
  • Garlic, pepper & salt
  • Vegetable oil

Preparation:

  1. Peel and fry the potatoes.
  2. Marinate chicken with spices.
  3. Grill until golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with ketchup or pepper sauce.

3. Amala n Ewedu

Ingredients:

  • Yam flour (Elubo)
  • Ewedu leaves
  • Seasoning cubes & salt
  • Palm oil & ground crayfish

Preparation:

  1. Cook ewedu leaves and blend.
  2. Boil with palm oil and spices.
  3. Prepare amala with hot water.
  4. Serve with your favorite stew.

4. Jollof Rice Special

Ingredients:

  • Rice & tomatoes
  • Pepper mix
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Vegetable oil

Preparation:

  1. Blend and fry pepper mix.
  2. Add spices and rice.
  3. Cook till rice absorbs the sauce.
  4. Serve with fried plantain or chicken.
